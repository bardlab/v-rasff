The Rapid Alert System for Food and Feed

RASFF, or the Rapid Alert System for Food and Feed, is the centralized system for reporting food-related safety issues withing the European Union. Its notifications are freely available to the public through a dedicated portal. This website aims at providing an effective visualization of said data, in order to enable citizens and professionals alike to make more efficient and instructive use of RASFF notifications.
